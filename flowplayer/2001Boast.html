2001 - Hal's Boast This scene is at the beginning of the third part of 2001. In it, HAL, the ship's computer, is being interviewed by a television reporter from the US. When questioned about his abilities, HAL boasts "Let me put it this way, Mr. Kramer. No 9000 computer has ever made a mistake or distorted information. We are, by any practical definitions of the words, foolproof and incapable of error."
