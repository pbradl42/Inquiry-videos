1964 Johnson Ad - Daisy The classic political advertisement in which a young girl picks the petals off a daisy, counting as she goes '10...9...8...' When she gets to '3', a loud voice takes over, and the ad ends with a nuclear blast. The ad ends by tell us to "Vote for President Johnson on Nov 3... The stakes are too high not to."
