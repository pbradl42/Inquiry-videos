1988 Dukakis Ad - One Beat Away Ad produced by Dukasis in the 1988 campaign 'criticizing' Bush for choosing Quayle as his running mate. There ad shows a photo of the oval office while a heartbeat plays in the background. The ad ends by reminding us that 1 in 5 VPs have had to step up to President, and "Hopefully, we'll never know how big a mistake" choosing Quayle was.
