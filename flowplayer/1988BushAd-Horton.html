1988 Bush Ad - Horton The ad that may have won the 1988 election for Bush the first. The ad claims that under Dukasis, Willie Horton was given a weekend pass from pris on, which he used to engage in the activities of "Kidnapping.. Stabbing... Raping". If we charitably allow it as an argument, it is a clear appeal to fear: vote for Bush or else a bunch of black men will be out stabbin' and rapin'!
